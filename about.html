About Me

Hi, I'm Austin. I am a Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Graphic Designer who loves creating digital experiences that are both engaging and easy to use. I enjoy turning ideas into websites, interfaces, and visuals that feel clean, functional, and visually appealing.

For me, great design is about more than just looks. It's about solving real problems and creating experiences that feel natural and intuitive. Whether I am building a responsive website, designing a user-friendly interface, or crafting a brand identity, I aim to deliver work that is high quality, practical, and meaningful.

I am comfortable working across Linux, macOS, and Windows and I enjoy exploring new tools and technologies, including AI, to make my workflow smarter and more creative. I also make sure every project is tested thoroughly so that the final result is polished and reliable.

If you are looking for someone who can bring both design and development together to create something memorable, I would love to connect and collaborate.
